## About Alexa
**Short Description:** Alexa is a voice-first personal assistant that helps people discover information, manage daily tasks, and seamlessly control smart environments.
### Overview
Alexa is a personal assistant that helps people get information and perform quick actions throughout their smart devices and the apps they use. People interact with Alexa primarily using their voice, creating an entirely hands-free experience across smart speakers, TVs, cars, and mobile apps.

On supported devices, Alexa uses secure cloud intelligence to process requests and manage connected environments. When developers integrate their features and devices with Alexa, people can use natural language to initiate actions, control smart home gadgets, and quickly access capabilities that would otherwise require navigating physical switches or multiple mobile menus.

**For example:**  You can say “Play my morning playlist” or “Set a kitchen timer for ten minutes” from across the room, and Alexa completes that task instantly. With smart lights connected, someone can say “Turn off the living room lights,” then follow up with “And lock the front door,” which secures the home without requiring manual inputs. Saying “What's on my calendar today?” to check a schedule can also be faster than opening a phone and scrolling through calendar views to find the same information.
