## Controlling smart home devices using Alexa
**Short Description:**  You can use Alexa to connect, manage, and automate your smart home devices, such as smart lights, plugs, thermostats, and locks, by using voice commands or the Amazon Alexa app.
### Prerequisites
Before you begin, make sure you have the following:
-	An active Amazon account that has access to Alexa features.
- The Amazon Alexa app installed on your mobile device.
- A compatible smart home device that is turned on, connected to your local network, and registered in its manufacturer app.

To connect and control a smart home device
Follow these steps to discover your device, configure its settings, and control it:

1 Open the Amazon Alexa app on your mobile device.

2 Select Devices from the bottom navigation menu, and then select the plus sign icon (+) in the upper-right corner.

3 Select Add Device, choose the category or brand of your device, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the pairing link.

> **Note:**  Many modern smart home devices support zero-touch setup and will connect automatically when plugged in near a compatible Echo device.

4 Organize your devices into locations by selecting Devices, selecting Groups, and adding the new device to a specific room, such as the Living Room or Kitchen.

5 Wake your device and state your request to control the smart home setup.
-	To change power states, say: "Alexa, turn on the Living Room light."
-	To adjust environment settings, say: "Alexa, set the thermostat to 72 degrees."
  
6 Confirm completion of the task by observing your physical device or listening for Alexa's response:
- Voice confirmation: "OK."
- Voice confirmation: "Setting the thermostat to 72 degrees."
